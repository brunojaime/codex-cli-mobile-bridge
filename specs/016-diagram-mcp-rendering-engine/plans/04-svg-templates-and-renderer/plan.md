# SVG Templates And Renderer

Define the deterministic SVG renderer and official UML templates for components, Provided/Required interfaces, connectors, labels, theme, scaling, and viewBox behavior.

Required outcomes:

1. The renderer produces SVG that is frontend agnostic and deterministic.
2. `uml_component` and `provided_required_interface` templates are specified with geometry and anchors.
3. Output SVG includes semantic IDs and can be displayed without frontend-specific drawing logic.
